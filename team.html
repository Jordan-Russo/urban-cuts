MEET THE TEAM

At Urban Cuts, we give an exceptional experience that leaves the men of Long Island looking and feeling their best.

Urban Cuts has been an integral part of the Suffolk community since it was founded in 1982. The barbershop was the brainchild of Zodd Gonzales, who had a vision of creating a space where people could come to not only get a great haircut but also feel like they were part of a community.

From its inception, Urban Cuts has been committed to providing top-quality grooming services to its clients. The barbershop has evolved over the years to keep up with changing trends and technologies, but its dedication to excellence has remained constant. Our experienced barbers have honed their craft over the years and have become trusted experts in the industry.

Looking to the future, Urban Cuts is committed to continuing its tradition of excellence. We are constantly exploring new trends and techniques in the industry to ensure that we remain at the forefront of the barbershop world. We envision a future where Urban Cuts continues to be the go-to destination for people looking for expert grooming services and a welcoming community.

We are excited to continue serving the Long Island community for many years to come. Whether you are looking for a classic cut or a modern style, our barbers are dedicated to creating a look that suits your unique style and personality. Come visit us at Urban Cuts and become part of our history as we move forward into an exciting future.
